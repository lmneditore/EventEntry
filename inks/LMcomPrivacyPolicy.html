LOUISVILLEMUSIC.COM PRIVACY POLICY

LouisvilleMusic.com ("The Company") is committed to respecting and protecting your privacy. This statement explains how The Company utilizes the personally identifiable information you provide online throughout the LouisvilleMusic.com/LouisvilleMusicNews.com Web site. It covers the five foundation principles of fair information: Awareness, Choice, Accuracy and Access, Security, and Oversight.

Awareness

This Privacy Statement is provided to make you aware of The Company's privacy policy, practices and of the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used.

What Information We Use and How We Use It

We use personal identifiable information that is collected through LouisvilleMusic.com/LouisvilleMusicNews.com such as name and email address so that we have a way to communicate with you regarding LouisvilleMusic.com's publications, products and offerings, to process your orders and regarding the content on LouisvilleMusic.com/LouisvilleMusicNews.com. The information is used to better understand your needs and provide you with better products and service; it is also used to ask you to participate in surveys about products, services, news and events and to provide you with targeted advertising, promotions, special offers and our products and services.

With Whom We Share It

We do not use your information for purposes incompatible with those listed above. Unless we have your permission or are required by law, we will only share the personally identifiable information you provide on LouisvilleMusic.com/LouisvilleMusicNews.com with other entities who are acting on our behalf for the uses described in this policy. Such entities shall not be allowed to retain any such information beyond its use as permitted by The Company.

Choice

Your personally identifiable information provided through LouisvilleMusic.com/LouisvilleMusicNews.com will not be used or shared by us in ways unrelated to the ones described above. We will also provide you the opportunity to let us know if you do not wish to receive unsolicited direct marketing materials from us and we will take reasonable steps within our control to honor such requests.

Accuracy & Access

In order to keep your personally identifiable information accurate, please follow the steps as identified in the Terms of Use posted at the www.LouisvilleMusic.com/LouisvilleMusicNews.com address. You will be provided access to your information so that you may view, update or correct the information you submitted.

Security

To prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the appropriate use of information, we use commercially reasonable efforts at our offices to safeguard and secure the information we obtain from our customers.

Oversight

If you have comments or questions about our privacy policy, please contact us at LouisvilleMusic.com, 3705 Fairway Lane, Louisville, KY 40207. We will work with you to address any issue you may have.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We revise this Privacy Statement from time to time to keep it up to date with our products and services. E-mail notifications or an "Updated" section on this policy may be used to alert users to recent changes and to the date of the last update. Please check this Privacy Statement from time to time.
